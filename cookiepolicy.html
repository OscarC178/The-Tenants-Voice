Cookie Policy

Last updated: September 30, 2025

Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Tenant's Voice ("we", "us", and "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. We use them to make our website work and to provide reporting information.

What we use and why

  • Strictly Necessary: We use essential cookies for core website functionality, like remembering your theme preference and saving your chat history in your browser's local storage. These cannot be disabled.
  • Performance and Analytics: If you consent, we use Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about how visitors use our site. This helps us understand which features are popular and how we can improve the user experience.
  • Advertising: We do not currently run ads, but we may in the future. If you consent, these cookies would be used to display relevant advertisements.

Your Consent

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through the banner on our site. Your preferences will be saved for future visits.
